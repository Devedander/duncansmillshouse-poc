Reservation Policies

  • You are reserving the entire house, not individual rooms.
  • The house comfortably sleeps up to 9 people (4 queen beds & 1 twin).
  • 4 bedrooms (one is a suite with a bathroom, kitchenette & deck), 3 total bathrooms, a full kitchen, dining area, living room, sitting room, office, decks, hot tub, BBQ, laundry room, and open space.
  • Two night minimum stay.
  • Reduced rates for longer stays — 10% off for a week, 15% off for a month.
  • Per Sonoma County regulations, TOT tax is 12% of the total price.
  • Cleaning fee is $350.
  • Events may be allowed — contact the owner to discuss.
  • Friendly pets may be allowed — contact the owner to discuss.
Rates range from $485 – $1,000 per night, depending on the season.
